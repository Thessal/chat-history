Meta-research on symbolic regression:
Disruptiveness and centrality of symbolic regression papers were calculated from citation network.
Purpose of this research is to find identify disruptive papers in this field.

Methodology:
Depth-2 reference tracking was applied with field constraint ( CS, Decision, Econ and Fin, Math, Physics )
Depth-0 papers were defined by condition "publication_year:2016-2026,title.search:review,cited_by_count:>100" with "symbolic regression" search query.
Citation network with 2129201 works and 6523689 citations were sampled from OpenAlex DB
Note: Bias was imposed by sampling method and not properly adjusted. (Publication year and outdegree)
Note: "keyword - Symbolic regression" match is too small. "topic - Evolutionary Algorithm" is used instead.
