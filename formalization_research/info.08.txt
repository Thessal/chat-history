title    : Ultrahigh Dimensional Feature Selection: Beyond The Linear Model
type     : article
year     : 2009
author   : j.fan r.samworth y.wu
openalex : W3002534707

* category : SIS(sure independ screening) is a method for sparse estimation of cov. mat.
* observation : Conventional sparse estimation method such as LASSO or Dantzig selector tends to overfit.
* observation : Magnitudes of factors chosen by LASSO is biased towards the regularization parameter \lambda by the definition. \lambda tends to be smaller because MSE = Bias^2 + Variance ~ \lamda^2 + Variance. Which in turn, high variance and overfit (selection of noise factor)
* definition : sure screening property implies the possibilty to exponential model size growth over sample count.
* definition : marginal utility is defined on a specific feature and the feature set, to measure the marginal loss reduction by the feature. this concept is similar to pareto optimimality argument. 
* hypothesis : two-stage approach with first step of filtering of factors based on marginal utility, and second step of factor selection and combination by pseudo-likelihood with penalty term, leads to sure screening property.
